1. Summary

U.N.C.L.E S.A.M stores only the data needed to operate features: Role IDs, Server IDs, User IDs, and Channel IDs. No message content is stored by default. Data is stored in MongoDB Atlas.

2. Data We Process

  • Operational Data: Role IDs, Server IDs, User IDs, Channel IDs.
  • Logs (transient): Standard server logs and error logs for reliability and abuse prevention.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Run and improve bot features.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues.
  • Comply with platform and legal requirements.

4. Legal Bases (where applicable)

  • Legitimate interests in providing and securing bot functionality.
  • Contract (your agreement to the Terms of Service by using the Bot).
  • Consent where required by local law.

5. Storage & Security

Data is stored in MongoDB Atlas with access control and industry-standard security practices. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data; however, no system is 100% secure.

6. Retention

Operational Data is retained for as long as the bot operates in your server and as necessary for functionality and security. When the bot is removed from a server, related Operational Data may be cleaned up during periodic maintenance cycles.

7. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with infrastructure/service providers strictly to operate the bot, and as required by law or to enforce our Terms.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and other locations where our providers operate. We rely on appropriate safeguards and agreements when required.

9. Your Choices

  • Remove the bot from your server to stop future processing.
  • Contact us for deletion or access requests where applicable.

10. Children

The Bot is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by your region). Do not use the Bot if you are under that age.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
